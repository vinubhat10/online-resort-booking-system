EVENTS



THE THREE PETAL BEACH RESORT offers a range of formal and informal Venues for destination weddings, events and meetings. Our Banquet halls and meeting rooms, along with the luscious green Lawns are perfect for hosting a range of corporate events, Meetings, product launches, training session... s, weddings, and so On. Give in to the gentle breeze and relaxed vibe of goa and leave The errands to our professional team of event planners,expert Staff and culinary team, who work together to plan and execute An immaculate ceremony to match your standards and Requirements.

At Three petals beach resort, we believe in making things exciting for the guests! Our well- trained and efficient team anticipates every need and fulfills all your wishes for any special occasion. From small intimate gatherings to large and lavish affairs, we ensure that every event is memorable, magical, perfectly planned and beautifully executed.
