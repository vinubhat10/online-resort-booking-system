DIFFERENT STYLES


Fettuccine Alfredo

ITALIAN

Fettuccine Alfredo or fettuccine al burro is an Italian pasta dish of fresh fettuccine tossed with butter and Parmesan cheese. As the cheese melts, it emulsifies the liquids to form a smooth and rich sauce coating the pasta.



CHINESE

Sichuan cuisine a style of Chinese cuisine originating from Sichuan Province. It has bold flavours, particularly the pungency and spiciness resulting from liberal use of garlic and chili peppers, as well as the unique flavour of Sichuan pepper. There are many local variations within Sichuan Province and the neighbouring Chongqing

Sichuan cuisine



Spicy tandoori chicken

INDIAN

Tandoori chicken is a chicken dish prepared by roasting chicken marinated in yogurt and spices in a tandoor, a cylindrical clay oven. The dish originated from the Indian subcontinent and is popular in many other parts of the world.



DISH TYPES




Amottik Fish Curry

This curry uses mostly shark fish or sting ray with typical Goan spices. Hot and sour/tangy, this fish curry reminds one of their grandmothers cooking. A total soul food.



Crab curry

The culture of Goa is an amalgamation of Indian and Portuguese culture.The long Portuguese rule has influenced the culture in more ways than one and many of the Portuguese traditions are reflected Fettuccine Alfredo Sichuan cuisine Spicy tandoori chicken in the Goan culture even today




Fish Temperade

Specially made of shellfish, crabs, prawns, mussels, clams etc., Fish Temperade is a popular dish available in fine restaurants across Goa.




Traditional Fish Curry

This would be the local favourite. Made of fish or prawns, green chilies or peppers (deseeded), onion, grated coconut, coriander seeds, cumin seeds, cloves garlic. Red dried chilies or peppers, ginger, turmeric powder and tamarind pulp Goes down well with rice and bread both.





Langouste Balchao

Many a times, this dish is passed off or confused withLobster Balchao. Langouste means Cray fish. Add oil, onion,green chilies, tomatoes, Recheiado masala, dried shrimps, sugar and salt to taste.

Arroz

This is made / consists of Basmati rice, ghee (clarified butter), onion, cloves, cinnamon, cardamoms, vegetable cubes, fresh lemon juice, olives and green peas.



Sannas

This is made / consists of rice, grated coconut, (with/without) toddy, sugar and salt to taste.




Vegetarian Dishes

Contrary to popular belief there are some very delicious vegetarian dishes which are very popular. Some local produce generally grown in the rice fields of Goa after harvesting are obviously what comes to the plate. Most of these vegetarian dishes like the non veg are cooked with grated coconut and sometimes with a sprinkling of tiny prawns/ dried prawns



DESSERTS


Bibinca

The most popular in Goa. It is made of coconut milk, yolks, flour, grated nutmeg, cardamom essence, and clarified butter (ghee).


Breadfruit curry

A favourite throughout the country, Breadfruit curry is made frequently in most homes in Goa. A large breadfruit is cooked with water,grated coconut, Goan spices, sugar and salt to taste.




Coconut Macaroons

Wonderfully crisp crust and deliciously sweet inside, Coconut macaroons are made of flour, salt, castor sugar, clarified butter (ghee), vanilla with a dash of almond essence



Dodol

A delightful toffee like sweetmeat, it is prepared of raw rice,grated coconut, jaggery (coconut molasses), cashew nuts, butter and vanilla essence.




Sans Rival

A delightful toffee like sweetmeat, it is prepared of raw rice,grated coconut, jaggery (coconut molasses), cashew nuts, butter and vanilla essence.

Goan beverages

Feni is the native alcoholic drink of Goa. Two types of Feni available are Coconut Feni made from toddy, the fermented sap of the coconut tree and Cashew Feni - the fermented juice of the cashew fruit.
